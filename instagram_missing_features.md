# Instagram Features Missing from Rooverse

> Based on a full audit of the codebase. Features already implemented are excluded.

---

## 🎬 Reels / Short-Form Video

- **Reels feed** — Dedicated full-screen vertical scrolling video feed (TikTok-style)
- **Reels creation tools** — In-app trimming, speed control (0.3x–3x), timer, countdown
- **Audio/music overlay** — Add trending audio tracks to videos
- **Remix / Collaborate** — Record a side-by-side reel reacting to someone else's reel
- **Reels templates** — Pre-made clip layouts to match a trending reel's structure
- **Reels insights** — Plays, reach, likes, comments per reel
- **Trending audio browser** — Browse and use popular audio clips

---

## 📡 Live Streaming

- **Go Live** — Broadcast live video to followers
- **Live co-host** — Invite another user to join your live stream
- **Live badges** — Viewers can buy and send badges to support creators
- **Live Q&A** — Pin viewer questions during a live
- **Live shopping** — Tag products during a live stream
- **Live replays** — Save and share a live video after it ends
- **Live notifications** — Followers notified when you go live

---

## 📸 Stories Enhancements

- **Story stickers** — Location, poll, quiz, slider, countdown, question box, link stickers
- **Story polls** — Binary choice polls with real-time results
- **Story question box** — Followers submit questions; answers shared in next story
- **Story quiz** — Multiple-choice quiz with correct answer reveal
- **Story countdown timer** — Countdown to an event with follow/remind option
- **Story link sticker** — Clickable link for all accounts (not just verified)
- **Story music sticker** — Add song with album art to story
- **Close Friends list** — Share stories only with a curated inner circle
- **Story highlights** — Pin selected stories to profile permanently (organized by category)
- **Story highlight covers** — Custom cover image per highlight reel
- **Story reshare** — Re-share a post or another story into your own story
- **Story reply reactions** — Quick emoji reactions to a story (fire, clap, etc.)
- **Story viewer list** — See exactly who viewed, in order
- **Draw / text tools** — Brush, pen, neon, highlighter, fonts, animated text
- **Story AR filters / effects** — Camera face filters and environment effects

---

## 📷 Camera & Creation Tools

- **In-app camera** — Dedicated camera with front/back flip, flash, grid overlay
- **Layout mode** — Combine up to 6 photos in a collage within a single post
- **Boomerang** — Short looping GIF-style video clips
- **Superzoom** — Dramatic zoom-in effects with sound effects
- **Hands-free video** — Record without holding button
- **Photo filters** — Real-time filter preview (Clarendon, Juno, Ludwig, etc.)
- **Photo editing tools** — Brightness, contrast, saturation, warmth, vignette, sharpen, fade
- **Video trim on upload** — Trim clips before posting
- **Multi-clip video** — Stitch multiple clips together for a single post
- **Alt text on posts** — Add accessibility description to images

---

## 🛍️ Shopping & Commerce

- **Instagram Shop tab** — Dedicated in-app storefront
- **Product tagging in posts** — Tag purchasable products directly on photos
- **Product tagging in stories** — Link to products in story stickers
- **Collection creation** — Curate saved products into named collections
- **Checkout in-app** — Buy products without leaving the app
- **Wishlist / product saves** — Save products for later
- **Shop from profile** — Seller profile with product catalog
- **Drop alerts** — Notify followers about upcoming product releases
- **Affiliate links** — Creators earn commission on tagged products

---

## 👥 Social & Discovery

- **Suggested users on profile** — "You might also like" panel on profile pages
- **Follow suggestions on feed** — Inline cards suggesting accounts to follow
- **Collab posts** — Co-author a post that appears on both users' profiles
- **Add yours sticker** — Chain-style contribution prompt in stories
- **Notes** — Short (60-char) disappearing status shown in DM inbox and follower list
- **Activity status** — "Active now" / "Active X minutes ago" in DMs
- **Mutual followers display** — "Followed by [name] + 3 others" on profile
- **Suggested searches** — Trending searches and autocomplete in Explore

---

## 💬 Messaging (DMs) Enhancements

- **Message reactions** — React to individual messages with emoji
- **Message reply threading** — Reply to a specific message in a thread
- **Disappearing messages** — Toggle view-once mode on photos/videos sent in DMs
- **Voice messages** — Record and send audio clips in DMs
- **Video call (1-on-1)** — In-app video calling from a DM thread
- **Group video call** — Video call with multiple participants
- **Voice call** — Audio-only calls
- **Vanish mode** — All messages disappear after being seen
- **Link previews** — Rich preview cards for URLs shared in DMs
- **Share posts to DM** — Forward a feed post directly into a conversation
- **Polls in DMs** — Create a poll inside a group DM
- **Payment in DMs** — Send money via integrated payment (US only on Instagram)
- **Chat themes** — Custom color/gradient themes per conversation
- **Pinned messages** — Pin important messages in a group chat
- **Chat requests** — Messages from non-followers go to a separate filtered inbox

---

## 🔍 Explore & Search

- **Explore grid** — Personalized 3-column grid of recommended posts/reels
- **Topic channels in Explore** — Filter Explore by category (Travel, Food, Beauty, etc.)
- **Reels tab in Explore** — Dedicated reels discovery section
- **Places search** — Find posts tagged at a specific location
- **Audio search** — Find all reels using a specific song/audio clip

---

## 👤 Profile Enhancements

- **Profile grid customization** — Pin a post to top of grid, reorder pinned posts
- **Pinned posts** — Highlight up to 3 posts at the top of your grid
- **Professional dashboard** — Unified view of insights, tools, and resources for creators
- **Creator/Business account types** — Switch between Personal, Creator, and Business
- **Contact buttons on profile** — Email, phone, or directions buttons for businesses
- **Category label** — Display job/niche label under username (e.g., "Musician", "Blogger")
- **Fundraiser link** — Add a fundraiser directly to profile
- **Avatar / cartoon profile pic** — Create a cartoon avatar to use as profile picture
- **Portfolio links** — Multiple clickable links in bio (link-in-bio style)

---

## 📊 Analytics & Insights

- **Account insights dashboard** — Reach, impressions, profile visits, follower growth over time
- **Content insights per post** — Reach, impressions, saves, shares breakdown per post
- **Story insights** — Exits, taps forward/back, link clicks per story frame
- **Reel insights** — Plays, accounts reached, watch time
- **Follower demographics** — Age range, gender, top locations, most active times
- **Best time to post** — Recommended posting windows based on audience activity

---

## 🔔 Notifications & Engagement

- **Notification categories** — Separate tabs for "From people you follow" vs "Everyone"
- **Close friends activity** — Priority notification lane for close friends' posts
- **Post reminder** — Set a reminder on a future-dated post or event

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Safety Enhancements

- **Hidden words filter** — Auto-hide comments/DMs containing specific words or phrases
- **Restrict accounts** — Limit an account without blocking (their comments hidden from others)
- **Limits mode** — Temporarily hide comments/DMs from non-followers or recent followers
- **Sensitive content control** — Slider to see less/more sensitive content in Explore
- **Comment filters** — Filter out offensive comments automatically
- **Who can tag/mention me** — Control who can tag or mention you in posts/stories
- **Story sharing controls** — Disable resharing of your stories
- **Older posts archiving** — Auto-archive posts older than X days
- **Account deletion grace period** — 30-day window to reactivate before permanent deletion

---

## ♿ Accessibility

- **Auto-generated captions on videos** — AI captions for Reels and stories
- **Alt text on images** — Manual or AI-generated alt text for screen readers
- **Captions sticker for stories** — Real-time speech-to-text captions on story videos

---

## 🌐 Other / Misc

- **Cross-posting to Facebook** — Share posts/stories simultaneously to a linked Facebook page
- **Broadcast channels** — One-to-many messaging channel (like a newsletter in DMs)
- **Subscriptions** — Paid monthly subscriber tier with exclusive content/badges
- **Gift sending** — Viewers send virtual gifts on Reels for creator revenue
- **Collab challenges** — Join a trending community challenge (hashtag + template)
- **QR code profile** — Scannable QR code that opens your profile
- **NFC profile share** — Tap phones to share profile
- **Threads integration** — Cross-post or link profile to Threads (text-based companion app)

---

> **Priority Recommendation:** Start with **Reels**, **Story stickers (polls/questions)**, **Story highlights**, **Voice messages in DMs**, and **Profile pinned posts** — these have the highest user engagement impact on Instagram and are missing here.
